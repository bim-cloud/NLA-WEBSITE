Infrastructure · Abu Dhabi · Phase 2

Mid Island Parkway

The Mid Island Parkway is a 25 km corridor, delivered in two phases, that traverses several islands and comprises dual carriageways, major interchanges, and service roads, organised into zones by geographical location.

Functionally, the Parkway establishes a corridor accommodating a high-quality transit route linking the central business districts of Abu Dhabi Island, while providing access to other major travel generators along it.

Key Design Objectives

  • Minimise environmental impacts
  • Achieve high sustainability levels
  • Consider freight and public transport requirements
  • Consider existing and future developments
  • Consider ongoing infrastructure projects
  • Achieve a high level of road safety
  • Optimise the delivery timeline

Landscape Design Focus

  • Establish a legible cyclist & pedestrian connection
  • Create interesting scenery for all road users — pedestrian, cyclist & vehicular
  • Contemplate how the landscape is viewed at different speeds
  • Address noise attenuation and visual barriers where required
  • Blend in with the surrounding natural landscape
  • Provide opportunities for people to connect with nature
